Our Mission

We want to give homeless people a platform to express themselves.

In a society where populations without social media can be massively underrepresented, many lack voices that they deserve to have. Through our platform, those more privileged can better understand what hardships homeless people face on a daily basis.

Bringing others together

Our idea allows volunteers throughout Seattle to go out and find stories from homeless people. From tough experiences to fun jokes, people will be more connected when they find more perspectives from someone new. No matter who you're talking to, you can be motivated, inspired, and gain a deeper understanding of the human experience.

Our hopeful results

We envision that society will be better educated about the underrepresented people around them. Through this, we will increase funding for homeless people, and in partnership with other Seattle homeless programs, we can tackle homelessness in an overall effort to keep people safe, sheltered, and set for a better future.

Why?

Our founders have personally interacted with homeless people numerous times in public spaces. Through these times, we've realized that these people have stories to tell that can influence millions of people. We want these stories to be heard, while also bringing the Seattle community closer together.
